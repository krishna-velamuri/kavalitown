Diet Plans

Blood Type Diet

The blood type diet based on our blood group. Blood type diet advices people to eat certain foods based on their blood type: A, B, AB or O. The plans posists that each blood type digests food proteins (lectins) differently and that eating the wrong food proteins can cause ill effects on the body such as like slower metabolism, bloating and even certain diseases. According to this diet, avoding bad food proteins will help you achieve better health.

GM Diet

The GM diet helps you to loss 3-8kg's in a week. This is very effective and easy to follow. This diet is restricted to take a few types of foods only. First day, all fruits except Banana. Second Day, all Vegitables except Potato. Third Day, Mix of Vegetable and Fruits (except: Banana and Potato). Fourth Day, 6 Bananas and two glasses of milk. Fifthe Day, 6 Tomatos and one cup of brown rice. Sixth Day, one cup of brown rice and vegitable. Last Day, one cup of brown rice, vegitables and fruit juices. Vegitable can be cooked (without oil) or raw. Daily 8-10 glasses water is mandatory.

Best Life Diet

The Best Life Diet attempts to change the way you living like eat, excercise etc. This diet goal is to tackle the root cause of overeating. The diet was designed to counter the quick-fix mentality of most diet programs out there. The best life diet promises to help you descover why you were out of shape to begin with and inspire physical and emotional changes that will last a lifetime.

3-hour Diet

The 3-hour diet involves eating small portions of food, every three hours throughout the day. The therory is that eating constantly will keep your metabolism continually at a high rate and burning fat. There are no prohibited types of food, only portion restrictions. Fried chicken, candy bars, bacon and red meat are allowed.

Atkins Diet

The atkins diet emphasizes eating lean protein and low-starch vegitables and avoding simple carbohydrates such as flour and sugar. The diet involves a reduced glycemic load(carbohydrates content) to keep blood sugar levels in healthy range. In theroy, consuming fewer carbohydrates allows your body to burn greater amounts of fat.

Cabbage Soup Diet

As its name suggests, the cabbage soup diet involves a very limited diet primarily made up of cabbage soup for a week in order to achieve quick weight loss. The diet promises rapid weight loss about 4kg in a week and is promoted as a vanity diet a quick way to lose a few extra kilograms to look good for a special occasion.

Caveman Diet

The caveman diet focuses on eating foods from the Paleolithic era of human nutritional needs. Foods we ate prior to farming and domesticating animals. The goal is to train your body to carve healthy foods. This diet promises to achieve your ideal body weight, sharpen the mind and enhance a connection to your bodies inner being like animals keen hunting instincts.

Fat Flush Diet

The Flat Fulsh Plan folds weight loss into a low-carbohydrate, restricted-calorie diet. The notion is that the liver is a "fat burning furnace", and the right combination of foods and a specific eating schedule will increase metabolism and cause the body to burn fat efficiently. The diet promises to clease the liver, which, in theroy, will help melt fat and cellulite away from the waist, hips and thighs.

Glycemic Index Diet

The Glycemic index(GI) is a scientific ranking that classifies foods based on how quickly they raise blood sugar levels. high-GI foods trigger a rise in blood sugar and release insulin, which is thought to trigger fat storage, intensify hunger, and lead to weight gain. The premise is that by avoiding food that have a hight GI score, your appitite will dicrease, and you will subsequently lose weight.
