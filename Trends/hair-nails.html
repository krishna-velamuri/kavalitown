Beauty tips for Hair-Nails

Hair Care

Hair is your crowning glory and hence, you can nott simply ignore them. Healthy hair is sign of health and beauty.

  • Never use hot or cold water for head bath, use warm water.
  • Never use hair dryer, follow natural ways.
  • Never brush wet hair.
  • Never use Chemical shampoo's. Do not believe T.V ad's look for ingredients charts back side of shampoo bottle/sachet.
  • Use cap or towel to cover hair while sleeping.
  • Water and sleep is very important. Drink at least 2lt water adn sleep for 8 hours.
