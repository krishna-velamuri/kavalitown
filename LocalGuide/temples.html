Sivalayam (Rudrapadha Kshetram)

Oldtown, Kavali -1

Sri Rudrapadha Bramarambika Mallikharjuna khsetram. This is one of the old temple in Kavali town. Monday is main day, will perform so many pooja, abhishek...etc There are many temples like Ayyappa, Sai baba, Ganapthi..etc adjacent to this temple. View is very good here. Swami pushkarini is very nice and big one. The paths around pushkarini planted and well maintained.

Sivarathri, Kartheeka masam celebrate...
