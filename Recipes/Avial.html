Avial

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 14 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 large capsicum-cut into 1" pieces
  2. 3 carrots-cut into thin fingers
  3. 10-12 french beans-1"pieces
  4. 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  5. 1 cup water
  6. 2 tbsp oil
  7. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  8. 1 tsp ginger
  9. 1/2 tsp garlic-chopped
  10. 1 large onion-chopped
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 2 tsp ground coriander
  13. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  14. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder (pasupu)
  15. 1 green chilli-cut into thin long pieces

Method

  1. Blend the grated coconut and water together in a mixer till smooth.
  2. keep the coconut milk aside.you can also use 1 cup of ready made coconut milk.
  3. Microwave oil,mustard seeds,chopped ginger,garlic,onion,dhania powder,garam masala,haldi and salt for 5 minutes.
  4. Add vegetables,mix and microwave for 3 minutes.
  5. Add the prepared coconut milk and green chilli.Microwave for 6 minutes.serve.
