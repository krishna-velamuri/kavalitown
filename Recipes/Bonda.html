Bonda

Preparation: 1 hour
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. Vegitables:Potato 1, Carrot 1,Beans 4, Capsicum 1 piece, Peas 1 tsp
  2. Chopped Green chillies 1 tsp
  3. Chopped Ginger 1 tsp
  4. Salt to taste
  5. Oil 1 tsp
  6. Mustard ½ tsp
  7. Turmeric powder ½ tsp
  8. Lemon juice 1 tsp(optional)
  9. Coriander leaves

Method

  1. Any or all of the above vegetables may be used.
  2. Cook the vegetables and mash them thoroughly.
  3. Heat 1 tsp oil, add mustard seed.
  4. When they splutter, add the green chillies, ginger and mashed vegetables, salt and turmeric powder. Mix well.
  5. Remove from stove. Add coriander leaves and lemon juice(this gives an extra tingling taste).
  6. Make small balls of the mixture, dip in the besan paste and deep fry.
