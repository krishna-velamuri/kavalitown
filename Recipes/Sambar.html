Sambar

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 14 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup red lentil - soak for 1 hour
  2. 100 gm pumpkin or 2 small brinjals (or any other vegitable for your choice) -1 cup
  3. lemon sized ball of tamarind - soaked in 1/4 cup hot water
  4. 1 tsp salt or to taste
  5. 1/4 tsp asafoetida powder
  6. 2 tbsp sambar powder
  7. 1 tbsp oil
  8. 1 onion - sliced
  9. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  10. a few curry leaves
  11. tiny piece of jaggery - optional

Method

  1. Put dal with 2 cups water in a deep bowl.
  2. Cover with cling film. Prick the film. Microwave for 15 mins or till dhal turns soft.
  3. Mash well. Extract tamarind pulp. Keep aside.
  4. Mix oil, curry leaves, saron, sambar powder and onions in a deep micro proof bowl.
  5. Microwave for 5 mins. Add chopped vegitables, salt, pureed dal and jaggery.
  6. Covered and microwave for 6 mins. Add 11/2 cups water and tamarind pulp.
  7. Microwave covered for 4 mins or till it boils.
  8. Uncover and microwave for 2 mins. Serve.
