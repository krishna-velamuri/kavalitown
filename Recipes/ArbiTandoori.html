Arbi Tandoori

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 18 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg colocasia (arbi) - medium size
MARINADE
  1. 1 cup curd - hang in a muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  2. 1 tsp ginger paste
  3. 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  4. 6-8 pepper corns - crushed
  5. 1 tsp oil
  6. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  7. 1/2 tsp chat masala
  8. 1/2 tsp carom seeds
  9. 1/4tsp turmeric
  10. 11/2 salt
  11. 2 tbsp gram flour

Method

  1. wash arbi. Spread wet arbi in a dish and cover with cling film.
  2. Microwave for 6 mins or till soft. Peel and cut each piece length wise into 2-3 slices.
  3. Lightly squeeze curd in the cloth. Add all ingredients of the marinade to the curd.
  4. Mix arbi in the marinade. At serving time, grease the non stick tawa.
  5. Place the arbi pieces on it. Place the tawa on high rack and grill for 8 mins or till golden.
  6. Turn side and grill for 4 mins. Serve with poodina chutney.
