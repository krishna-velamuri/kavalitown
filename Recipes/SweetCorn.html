Sweet Corn

Preparation: 8 mins
Cooking: 9 mins

Ingredients

  1. 75 gm chicken with bones (1-2 pieces)
  2. 2 cups water
  3. ¾ tsp salt
  4. 1whole corn-on-the cob
  5. 1 tbsp cabbage- shredded
  6. 1 tbsp grated carrot
  7. ½ tsp red or chilli sauce
  8. 2 tsp white vinegar
  9. 1 tsp sugar
  10. ¼ tsp white pepper some black pepper
  11. ½ tsp ajinomoto (optional)
  12. 3 tbsp corn flour – dissolve in ¼ cup water

Method

  1. Wash corn with leaves to wet it. Microwave for 3 minutes. Scrape corn kernels with a knife.
  2. Grind ½ cup water till smooth. Keep some corn whole.
  3. Microwave water, chicken and salt in a covered dish for 6 minutes.
  4. Remove flesh from chicken pieces. Keep liquid a side. Discard bones.
  5. Put corn, stock, chilly sauce, sugar, pepper and ajinomoto. Microwave covered for 4 minutes.
  6. Add corn flour paste, cabbage, and carrot. Microwave uncovered for 6 minutes.
  7. Add chicken and vinegar. Sprinkle black pepper. Serve hot.
