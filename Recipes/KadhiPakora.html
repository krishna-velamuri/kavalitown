Kadhi Pakora

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 13 1/2 mins

Ingredients

PAKORAS
  1. 1/2 cup gram flour - sifted
  2. 1/2 onion & potato - finely chopped
  3. a pinch of mitha soda
  4. 1/4 cup tsp baking powder
  5. 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  6. 1/2 tsp chopped ginger
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 1/4 tsp garam masala
  9. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
KADHI
  1. 1/4 cup gram flour
  2. 1 cup curd c
  3. 3 cups water
  4. 1/4 tsp turmeric
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp chilli powder
TEMPER
  1. 2 tbsp oil
  2. 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  3. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  4. 1-2 red chillies

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients of pakoras, adding water very gradually to get a thik soft paste of dropping consistency.
  2. Beat for 2 mins till light and fluffy. Grease a flat dish.
  3. Put pakora batter with a tsp in the dish, leaving some space in between. Microwave for 11/2 mins.
  4. Mix all ingredients of kadhi till smooth. Strain into a deep bowl.
  5. Microwave for 10 mins. Stirring once in between. Add pakoras.
  6. For tempering, mix oil with cumin and mustard. Microwave for 2 mins.
  7. Add red chillies to hot oil. Pour over the kadai. Serve.
