Murg Pulao

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 16 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup basmati rice
  2. 250 gm chicken - cut in to pieces
  3. 3/4 tsp salt
  4. 1/2 tsp haldi (pasupu)
PASTE
  1. 3 dried red chillies - dressed
  2. 2 onion - chopped
  3. 3 tbsp oil
  4. 12-14 flakes garlic
  5. 2 tsp chopped ginger
  6. 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  7. 2 tsp fennel
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 1" stick cinnamon (dalchini)
  10. seeds of 2 black cardamoms
  11. 3 laung
  12. 8 pepperconrns

Method

  1. Soak rice in 2 cups water for 30 mins. For the paste grind all ingrediants together, using some water.
  2. Mix chicken and the prepared paste in a big micro proof dish. Cover and microwave for 3 mins.
  3. Add rice along with the water. Add salt and haldi. Cover and microwave for 13 mins.
  4. Separate the grains with a fork. Serve after 5 mins.
