Benarasi Kheer

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 34 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 ltr milk (5 cups)
  2. 1/3 cup rice-wash and soak for 1 hours
  3. 1/2 tin milk maid
  4. 1/2 tsp saffron
  5. 10 almonds(badam)-blanched and chopped
  6. 10-15 raisins (kismis)-soaked in water & squeezed
  7. 2-3 green cardmoms (elachi)

Method

  1. Microwave covered milk and rice together for 9 mins. Stir once. Microwave uncovered for 5 mins.
  2. Add milk maid and kesar. Mix well.
  3. Microwave uncovered for 10 mins. Stir in between.
  4. Microwave at 60%(540 w)power for 5 mins. Remove form oven and mash well with a potato masher to break rice.
  5. Microwave for 5 mins at 60%(540 w) power. Add almonds and raisins. Cool.Add elachi powder.
  6. Chill and serve.
