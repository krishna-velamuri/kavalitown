Fish Tikka

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 8 mins

Ingredients

  1. 500 gm boneless Singhara or Sole fish - cut into 2" cubes
  2. 3 tbsp gram folur
  3. 1 tbsp lemon juice
1st MARINADE
  1. 2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  2. 1/4 tsp salt
  3. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
2nd MARINADE
  1. 3/4 cup curd - hang in muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  2. 1/2 tsp carom seeds
  3. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1/4 cup thick cream
  5. 3/4 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp pepper
  7. 1 tbsp corn flour
  8. 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  9. 2 tsp dry fenugreek leaves

Method

  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken legs. Make 2 light cuts/incissions on each drumstick.
  2. Marinate chicken with 1st marinade for 30 mins.
  3. Mix all ingredients of 2nd marinade in abowl. Add only chicken pieces discarding the liquid.
  4. Marinate for 30 mins. To server, grease a flat borosil dish. Place the chicken on it.
  5. Cook on combination mode for 8 mins or till golden. Turn side and smear the remaining marinade.
  6. Cook for 4 mins on the same combination mode. Serve with chutney.
