Vegitable Soup

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup carrots (chopped)
  2. 1/4 cup french beans (chopped)
  3. 1/2 cup peas (shelled)
  4. 1 1/2 cup tomato puree
  5. 1 tsp garlic paste
  6. 1 tsp ginger paste
  7. 2 tsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  8. 3-4 tsp butter
  9. 3 glasses of water
  10. salt and pepper to taste
  11. cheese granted

Method

  1. Melt butter, fry ginger and garlic paste, add tomato puree, carrots, French beans, peas and water.
  2. ressure cook for 5-6 minutes.
  3. Add coriander leaves. Boil for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Garnish with cheese.
    5. Note: If you want thick soup, then add 2 tsp of corn flour mixed in 1/2 cup milk.
