Corn Upma

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 15 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup semolina(rawa suji)
  2. 4 tbsp oil
  3. 1 tbsp mustard seeds(sarson)
  4. a few curry leaves
  5. 2 green chillies-chopped
  6. 1 big onion-finely chopped
  7. 1/2 cup corn
  8. 1/4 cup chopped beans
  9. 2 3/4 cups water
  10. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  11. 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  12. salt to taste

Method

  1. Mix 3 tbsp oil&suji in a flat glass dish.Spread evently at the bottom of the dish and microwave for 2 1/2 minitues.
  2. Mix again and microwave for another 2 minutes.Remove and immediately mix well.Keep aside.
  3. In a separate dish,put 1 tbsp oil,Add sarson,curry leaves,green chillies,onion and beans.
  4. icrowave for 4 1/2 minutes.
  5. Add salt,red chilli powder,water,corn and lemon juice gradually add suji,stirring constantly.
  6. Microwave covered for 4 minutes.Stair.
  7. Remove cover and microwave for 1 1/2 minutes.Serve.
