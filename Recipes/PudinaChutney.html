Tamota Chutney

Pudina Chutney

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 15 mins

Ingredients

  1. Mint leaves - 1 cup tightly packed
  2. Pearl onions - 7-8
  3. Green chilli -2
  4. Grated Coconut -1/4 cup
  5. Tamarind - a small piece
  6. Salt as required
For the Seasoning
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. 3/4 tsp urad dhal(black gram)
  3. 3/4 tsp Mustard

Method

  1. Remove stalks and take the mint leaves alone. Wash the mint leaves and keep it aside. Drain the water well.
  2. Heat 2-3 tsp of oil , add pearl onions and fry till it turns pink.
  3. Then add green chilli and saute for a few more seconds .
  4. Now switch off the flame and add the mint leaves. fry in the heat of the pan itself. Add grated coconut and mix well.
  5. Grind everything with needed salt, tamarind and very less water.
  6. Heat a tsp of oil, add mustard seeds, when mustard splutters, add urad dal, when dal turns golden brown, pour the seasoning over the chutney.
