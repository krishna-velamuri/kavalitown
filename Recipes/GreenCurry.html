Thai Green Curry

Preparation: 20 mins
Cooking: 12 mins

Ingredients

  1. 100 gm baby corn- cut into half
  2. 8-10 florets of brocoli, 1/2 carrot sliced
  3. 11/2 tsp brown sugar or jaggery
  4. 2 tbsp oil
  5. 2 cups coconut milk
GREEN CURRY PASTE
  1. 5 green chilies and 1/2 onion - chopped
  2. 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  3. 1/2" piece ginger
  4. 1 lemon grass stalk - chopped
  5. 1/2 cup basil or coriander
  6. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  7. 1 tsp coriander seeds
  8. 1/2 tsp pepper corns
  9. 1/4 tsp grated nutmug
  10. 1/2 tsp salt

Method

  1. Prepare the green curry paste by grinding everything together with about 1/2 cup water.
  2. In amicro proof dish add green paste and oil. Mix well.
  3. Microwave uncovered for 3 mins. Add vegitables. Mix well. Microwave for 2 mins
  4. Add sugar and coconut milk. Mix well. Microwave uncovered for 6 mins.
  5. Let it stand for 3 mins. Check salt. Serve garnished with basil leaves.
