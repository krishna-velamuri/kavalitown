Thai Red Curry

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 12 mins

Ingredients

RED CURRY PASTE
  1. 4-5 dry kashmiri red chilli
  2. 1/2 onion
  3. 8-10 flakes garlic
  4. 1 tsp chopped ginger
  5. 1 stalk lemon grass or rind of 1 lemon
  6. 11/2 tsp coriander seeds
  7. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  8. 6 saboot kali mirch
  9. 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  10. 1 tsp lemon juice
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 1 tsp sugar
OTHER INGREDIENTS
  1. 4-5 baby corns
  2. 1/2 cup florets of brocoli or cauliflower
  3. 2 mushrooms
  4. 8-10 basil leaves
  5. 2 cups ready made coconut milk

Method

  1. Microwave red chillies with 1/2 cup water for 2 mins.
  2. Add all the other ingredients of red curry paste and grind to a very fine red paste.
  3. Slice carrots, baby corns and mushrooms.
  4. Mix 2 tbsp oil and red curry paste in a micro proof dish. Microwave for 2 mins.
  5. Add coconut milk, vegitables& microwave for 6 mins or till veggies are crisp tender.
  6. Add salt and sugar to taste. Add basil leaves.
  7. Microwave for 2 mins. Serve hot with steamed rice.
