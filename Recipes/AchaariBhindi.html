Achaari Bhindi

Preparation: 20mins
Cooking: 17 1/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 500 gm lady fingers(bhindi)
  2. 1 tsp ginger paste
  3. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  4. 1 tsp dhania powder
  5. 1/2 tsp amchoor
  6. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  7. 3/4 tsp salt,or to taste
  8. 2 tomotoes-chopped finely
  9. 1 tsp lemon juice
  10. ACHAARI SPICES
  11. a pinch of asafoetida(hing)
  12. 1 tsp fennel(saunf)
  13. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds(rai)
  14. 1/2 tsp onion seeds (kalonji)
  15. 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Wash bhindi and wipe dry.now cut the bhindi lengthwise from the middle making.
  2. thin pieces from each. 2.Grease the non stick tawa.place the bhindi pieces on it.
  3. Sprinkle 1 tbsp oil and mix well.Spread bhindi in a single layer.
  4. Place the tawa on high rack and grill for 8 minutes.Mix lightly and grill for 2 minutes.Transfer to a glass dish.
  5. In a small bowl put 2 tbsp oil and all the achaari spices.Microwave for 2 1/2 minutes.
  6. To the bhindi,add achaari spices and all the remaining ingredients.Mix very well.Microwave for 5 minutes. Serve
