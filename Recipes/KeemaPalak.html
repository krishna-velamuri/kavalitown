Keema Palak

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 18 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg chicken/mutton mince(keema)-wash in a soup strainer
  2. 1 1/2 cups shredded spinach(palak)
  3. 3 tbsp butter,2 onions-chopped
  4. 1/4 tsp haldi(pasupu)
  5. 1 tsp cumin seeds(jeera)
  6. 1/4 tsp nigella seeds(kalonji)
  7. 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds(methi)
  8. 1/2 tsp fennel seeds(saunf)-crushed
  9. Tomato paste
  10. 2 tomatoes
  11. 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  12. 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  13. 2 dry red chillies
  14. 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Put buttor in a micro proof dish and microwave for 30 seconds.
  2. Add jeera,kalonji,methi dana,saunf,haldi and onions.Mix well.Microwave for 5 minutes.
  3. Add keema,mix well.
  4. Add shredded palak and the prepared tomoto paste. Mix and microwave for 5 minutes.Stir once in between.
  5. Check salt,add more if required.Mix and microwave for 5 minutes or till almost dry.
  6. Let it stand for 5 minutes.Serve.
