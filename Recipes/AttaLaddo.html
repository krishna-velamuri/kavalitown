Atta Laddo

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 71/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup whole wheat flour
  2. 1/3 powdered sugar
  3. 3-4 tbsp ghee
  4. 1 tbsp dry fruits chopped
  5. seed of one green cardamom - crushed
  6. 2 pinches black pepper
  7. 2 pinches garam masala powder
  8. 2 pinches dry ginger powder

Method

  1. Spread atta in a flat dish and microwave for 4 mins at 720W to dry roast the atta.
  2. Stri once between. Add ghee and mix very well.
  3. Microwave for 2 mins at 540W. Mix well.
  4. Microwave for 1 1/2 mins more at 540W. Mix well.
  5. Add sugar to taste. Add dry fruits and powders.
  6. Mix very well, make small laddoos.
