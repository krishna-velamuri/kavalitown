Subz Pulao

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 16 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup basmati rice
  2. 3 tbsp oil
  3. 1 tsp ginger paste
  4. 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  5. 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  6. 1 potato - cut into 1/2" pieces
  7. 2 carrots - cut into slices
  8. 1 cup green peas
  9. 1/4 tsp pasupu
  10. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  11. 1 1/2 tsp salt
  12. 1 tbsp mint leaves
  13. 1 tsp lemon juice
SPICE BAG (Crush the spice lightly)
  1. 10 pepper corns
  2. 2 tsp fennel sees
  3. 3-4 green cardamoms
  4. 3-4 black cardamons
  5. 4 cloves
  6. 2 sticks cinnamon

Method

  1. Soak rice in 2 cups water for 30 mins. Tie the ingradiants of the spice bag in a piece of muslin cloth.
  2. In a broad micro proof dish, put oil, ginger, garlic, cauliflower,potato, carrots and peas. Microwave for 3 mins.
  3. Add the soaked rice. Add haldi, red chilli powder and salt.
  4. Add the spice bag. Microwave covered for 6 mins. Add mint and lemon juice.
  5. Stri gently with a fork. Cover and microwave for 7 mins. Wait for 5 mins. Fluff with a fork.
  6. Remove spice bag. Serve hot.
