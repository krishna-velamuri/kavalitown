Crispy Chicken

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 4 mins

Ingredients

  1. 500 gm small chicken legs (5 drumsticks)
  2. 1 egg
  3. 1/4 tsp salt
  4. 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  5. 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  6. 1-2 tbsp oil-sprinkle
MARINATE
  1. 2 tbsp oil or melted butter
  2. 2 tsp garlic paste
  3. 11/2 tsp chilli powder
  4. 1 tsp cumin seeds powder
  5. 2 tsp coriander powder
  6. 2" stick cinnamon(dalchini) - crushed (1 tsp)
  7. 1 1/2 tsp salt

Method

  1. Prick chicken with a fork all over. mix all marinade ingradients.
  2. Add chicken. Keep covered for 4 hours or overnight in the fridge.
  3. Cover with a plastic wrap and microwave for 4 minutes.
  4. Dip each chicken leg in egg and roll it in bread crumbs.
  5. Place chicken on the nonstick tawa placed on a low rack.
  6. Pour 1 tbsp oil or melted butter on the pieces and grill for 8 minutes.
  7. Turn pieces and brush 1 tbsp oil. Grill for 4 mins or till chicken turns golden. Serve.
