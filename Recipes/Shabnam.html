Shabnam Curry

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 17 mins

Ingredients

  1. 200 gm mushrooms - each cut into 4 pieces
  2. 1/2 cup peas - frozen or boiled
  3. 1/2 cup corn (forzen or boiled)
  4. 1/4 cup cahews
  5. 1/2 cup curd
  6. 3 tbsp oil
  7. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  8. 2 onions - ground to paste
  9. 1/4 tsp nigella seeds
  10. 1/2 tsp jeera
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  13. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  14. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  15. 1 tsp kasoori methi
  16. 2 tamatos - pured and sieved
  17. 2 cloves
  18. 2 cardmom( crushed both cloves and cardmom)

Method

  1. Grind cashews and curd to a paste. Puree tomatos.
  2. Strain tomato puree and keep aside.
  3. Mix oil, onions, kalonji, jeera, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli, haldi, garam masala and salt in a dish.
  4. Microwave for 5 mins. Add pured and sieved tomatoes and kasoori methi.
  5. Microwave for 4 mins. Add 1 cup water and mushrooms. Microwave covered for 5 mins
  6. Add peas, corn, crushed clove and cardmom. Add kaju paste.
  7. Uncover and microwave for 3 mins. Serve hot.
