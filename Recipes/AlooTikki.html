Aloo Tikki

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 19 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg (6 medium)potatoes
  2. 1/2 cup boiled or frozen peas
  3. 1 tbsp salt
  4. 1 tbsp oil
  5. 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin(jeera powder)
  6. 2 tsp finely chopped ginger
  7. 2 tsp chopped green chillies
  8. 2 tbsp chopped corinder leaves
  9. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  10. 1 tsp chaat masala
  11. 1/4 tsp garam masala

Method

  1. Wash and put wet potatoes in a micro safe steamer .Microwave covered for 5 minutes.Cool.
  2. Peel and mash potatoes.Add oil,peas,salt,jeere,chopped green chillies,ginger,coriander, red chilli powder,chaat masala and garam masala.
  3. Make tikkis of 3" diameter.
  4. Brush the non stick tawa of the microwave with some oil.Arrange tikkis on it.
  5. Brush the tikkis with oil.Place the tawa on a high rack.
  6. Grill for 10 minutes.Turn side and grill for 4 minutes.
