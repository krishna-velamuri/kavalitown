Dhania Machhi

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 4 mins

Ingredients

  1. 250 gm boneless fish (preferably Sole) - cut into 11/2" square pieces of 1/2" thickness
  2. 1/4 tsp salt
  3. juice of 1 lemon
PASTE
  1. 4" piece of coconut chopped
  2. 1/2 cup coriander - chopped
  3. 2 flakes garlic - chopped
  4. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  5. 1/4 tsp sugar
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 2-3 tbsp water - to grind

Method

  1. Wash the fish. Rub lemon and salt and leave for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Wash the fish again and pat dry on a kitchen towel.
  3. Grind the ingredients of the paste coarsely in a mixer.
  4. Smear each piece of fish with paste on both sides.
  5. Marinate for 1 hour or till serving time in a micro proof dish.
  6. Microwave covered for 31/2 mins. Give standing time of 1 minute. Serve.
