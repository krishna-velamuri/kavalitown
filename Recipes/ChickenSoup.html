Chicken Soup

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 17 mins

Ingredients

  1. 75 gms chicken cut into 3-3 pieces
  2. 31/2 cups water
  3. ½ tsp salt
  4. 1 tbsp oil
  5. ½ tsp crushed garlic
  6. ½ tsp red chili paste
  7. 2 tbsp chopped cabbage
  8. 2 tbsp grated carrot
  9. 1 chicken soup cube-crushed
  10. 1 tsp sugar
  11. ½ tbsp pepper
  12. ½ tbsp soya sauce
  13. 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  14. 1 tbsp white vinegar
  15. 4 tbsp corn flour : dissolve in ½ cup water

Method

  1. For stock, microwave covered 31/2 cups water with chicken and salt in a deep micro proof bowl for 8 minutes.
  2. Pick up chicken pieces. Strain the stock through a fine strainer to get a clear stock.
  3. Remove bones from the chicken. Keep shredded chicken and stock aside.
  4. In the same bowl put 1 tbsp oil. Add red chilli paste and garlic. Microwave covered for ½ minute.
  5. Add stock and all other ingredients except corn flour paste. Microwave covered for 6 minutes.
  6. Add corn flour paste and chicken. Microwave covered for 3 minutes or till it boils. Stir once in between.
