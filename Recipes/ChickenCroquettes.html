Chicken Croquettes

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 200 gm chicke with bones
  2. 1 tbsp oil
  3. 1 tbsp softned butter
  4. 1/2 onion
  5. 2 green chillies - chopped
  6. 1/2 tbsp flour (maida)
  7. 1/4 cup milk
  8. 1 bread slice - grind to get fresh crumbs
  9. 1/4 tsp red chilli flakes
  10. 1/2 tsp salt
  11. 1/4 tsp pepper
  12. 1 egg (separate and keep 2 tbsp white to coat)
TO COAT
  1. 2 tbsp water mixed with 2 tbsp egg white
  2. 1/4 cup flour (maida)
  3. 1 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Mix chicken and oil in a dish. Microwave covered for 4 mins.
  2. Shred chicken to tiny pieces. Discard dones.
  3. Mix butter, onion, chillies and maida in a dish. Microwave for 1 min.
  4. Mix milk and microwave uncovered for 1 min. Add all the other ingradients.
  5. Mix well and shape into rolls.
  6. Dip the rolls in egg mixture and roll over maida. Grease a non stick tawa with oil.
  7. Place the rolls on it. Brush some oil on the rolls. Place the tawa on the high rack and grill for 10 mins.
  8. Change side and grill for 4 mins till the rolls turn golden. Serve topped with mayonnaise.
