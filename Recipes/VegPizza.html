Veg Pizza

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 ready-made pizza bases
  2. 100-150 gm mozzarella or pizza chease grated(1-11/2 cup)
  3. 1/2 onion
  4. 1/2 yellow or green capsicum cut into 1/2" squares
  5. a few olives
  6. 1 tomato - cut in to 8 pieces
  7. 2 tbsp corn kernels or 3-4 baby corns cut into thin long slices
TOMATO SPREAD
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. 1 tsp garlic paste
  3. 1/2 cup ready-made tomato puree
  4. 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  5. 1 tsp orengano
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 1/4 tsp pepper

Method

  1. For tomato spread, put all ingredients of the tomato spread in a bowl and microwave for 21/2 minutes.
  2. Spead on each pizza base, leaving the edges.
  3. Arrange some vegitables. Spinkle some salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and oregano.
  5. Arrange a few vegitable on top. Place the pizza on the low rack.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven pre heated at 250°C and bake for 8 mins.
  7. First brush bottom of pizza base with oil and grill.
  8. With bottom side up on the greased tawa place on a high rack for 3 mins till crisp.
  9. Put tomato spread and the topping. Grill again for 4 minutes.
Note: Alternately, you can use grill mode also.
