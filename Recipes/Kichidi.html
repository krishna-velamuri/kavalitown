Kichidi

Preparation: 1 hour
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup white basmati rice, washed in water and drained
  2. 1 cup yellow lentils(Pesara pappu), washed and soaked in water and drained
  3. About 4 cups of water
  4. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  5. 1 tsp oil
  6. Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot. (Preferably one with a cover).
  2. When oil is hot, add cumin seeds. Allow cumin seeds to splutter but do not let them get burnt.
  3. Fold in the yellow lentils and rice. Mix cumin seeds thoroughly with the lentils and rice.
  4. Bring to boil. Lower the heat, partially cover the pot and cook until the rice is cooked.
  5. Serve hot with a sprinkling of coriander.
  6. This dish can also be served with some plain yoghurt.
