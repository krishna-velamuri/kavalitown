Paneer Tikka

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 9 mins

Ingredients

  1. 400 gm paneer - cut into 11/2" long and 1/2" thickness
  2. 1 cup curd - hang in a muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  3. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  4. 11/2 tsp corn flour
  5. 1/4 tsp turmeric
  6. 1 tsp oil
  7. 1 tbsp tandoori masala or 1/2 tsp garam masala
  8. 1/2 tsp chat masala
  9. 11/4 tsp salt or to taste
  10. 1 tbsp dry fenugreek leaves
  11. 1/2 tsp degi mirchi
  12. some extra chat masala to sprinkle

Method

  1. Beat curd till smooth. Add all ingredients except paneer to the curd.
  2. Add the paneer cubes to the curd. Marinate till serving time.
  3. At serving time, grease the non stick tawa. Place paneer pieces on it.
  4. Place tawa on hight rack and grill paneer for 8 mins. Turn side once in between.
  5. If the tikkas are not golden brown, grill for 1-2 mins more.
  6. Serve sprinkled with chat masala.
