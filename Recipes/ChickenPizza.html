Chicken Pizza

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 11 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 ready made pizza bases
  2. 150 gm pizza cheese -grated
MARINATE FOR 30 MINUTES
  1. 1 boneless chicken breast (150 gm) - cut into 1/4" pieces
  2. 1 tsp red chilli paste
  3. 1/2 onion
  4. 1/2 capsicum - chopped
  5. 1 tsp soya sauce
  6. 1 tsp vinegar
  7. a pinch of ajinomoto (optional)
  8. 1/2 tsp pepper
  9. 1/2 tsp salt
  10. 1/2 tsp oregano
  11. 1 tsp cornflour
  12. 1 tbsp oil
  13. 4 flakes garlic - chopped
  14. 1/2 cup ready made tomato puree
  15. 3 tbsp tomato ketchup

Method

  1. In a dish marinate chicken with all ingredients for 1/2 hour or even more.
  2. Cover chicken. Microwave for 3 mins.
  3. To assemble, place pizza on a wire rack or greased tawa.
  4. Sprinkle some cheese. Spread chilli chicken. Sprinkle the remaining cheese.
  5. Put pizza in the oven preheated at 250°c and bake for 8 mins or till the base gets crisp amd the cheese melts.
  6. Alternately brush the bottom of the pizza base with oil and place with bottom side up on the greased tawa placed on a high rack.
  7. Grill for 3 mins till crisp. Put topping on the other side and grill again for 4 mins.
