Sweet and Sour Veggie

Preparation: 20 mins
Cooking: 15 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup pineapple juice or syrup
  2. 3-4 baby corns - cut into 2 lengthwise
  3. 4-5 medium floaters broccoli or cauliflower
  4. 3-4 mushrooms - cut into 2 pieces
  5. 2 slices pineapple - fresh or tinted
  6. 1 small capsicum - cut into 1/2" pieces
  7. 1/2 tsp red chilli paste
  8. 11/2 tsp crushed garlic
  9. 2-3 spring onions white - cut into slices
  10. 1/4 cup tomato ketchup
  11. 3 tbsp vinegar
  12. 1/4 tsp ajinomoto
  13. 4 tsp sugar
  14. 1 tsp soya sauce
  15. 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  16. 1 stock cube - crushed
  17. 4 tbsp corn flour mixed in 1/4 cup water

Method

  1. Microwave covered 11/2 cups water, 1/2 cup pineapple juice, baby corns, floaters and mushrooms for 6 mins.
  2. Add tomato ketchup, vinegar, ajinomoto, sugar, soya sauce, salt and stock cube.
  3. Add the remaining vegitables 1" pieces of pineapple and capsicum to the above vegitables. Keep aside.
  4. Microwave 2 tbsp oil, onions, garlic, red chilli paste in a separate bowl for 3 mins.
  5. Add the onions to the vegitables. Add the cornflour paste also.
  6. Microwave for 6 mins stirring once in between. Serve.
