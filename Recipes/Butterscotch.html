Butterscotch Pastry

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 6 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cake base
CREAM ICING
  1. 300 gm cream
  2. 1/2 cup icing sugar
  3. 2 tbsp butterscotch essense
  4. 2 tbsp oil
COFFEE SYRUP
  1. 1 cup water
  2. 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  3. 2 tsp coffee
  4. 1 tsp butterscotch essense
OTHER INGREDIENTS
  1. 100 gm cooking chocolate - scrape with a peeler to get a few curls, then cut the rest into pieces
  2. 2 tsp coffee powder mixed with 2 tsp water

Method

  1. Microwave a chocolate cake in a 10" square dish for 5 mins.
  2. Cut into two horizontally when it cools Beat cream till slightly thick.
  3. Add icing sugar and essense. Beat till soft peaks form.
  4. Microwave chocolate pieces in a bowl for 1 min. Mix well to make it smooth.
  5. Add 1 cup of whipped cream to get choco-cream. To the remaining cream, add 2 tsp coffee mixed with water to get coffee cream.
  6. Serve topped with a chocolate curl.
