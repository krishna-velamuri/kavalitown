Sukhi Dhuli

Preparation: 1 hour
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup split black gram-soaked in 1 1/2 cups water for 1 hour
  2. 1 onion-sliced
  3. 1 piece ginger-grated
  4. 3 tbsp oil
  5. 3/4 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp turmeric(pasupu)
  7. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. 1/4 tsp amchoor
  9. 1 tsp dhania powder

Method

  1. Clean and wash dal.soak in 1 1/2 cups water for 1 hour.
  2. Keep onion & ginger in a flat round glass dish.sprinkle oil on it.Mix.
  3. Add powder,amchoor and dhania powder. Microwave uncovered for 5 minutes.
  4. Add dal along with water to the onions. Mix well.
  5. Microwave covered for 15 minutes.Stir once in between after 8 minutes.
  6. Let it stand for 3 minutes. Sprinkle chopped coriander and mix gently with a fork.Serve hot.
