Chilli Prawns

Preparation: 2 mins
Cooking: 3 mins

Ingredients

  1. 250 gm (7-8) medium size prawns
  2. 1 tsp red chilli paste
  3. 1/4 freshly crushed black pepper
  4. a pinch of sugar
  5. 6-8 flakes garlic - crushed& chopped finely (1 tbsp)
  6. 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  7. 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  8. 1 tbsp oil
  9. 1 tsp soya sauce
  10. 1 tbsp corn flour
  11. 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Wash and clean the prawns. Pat dry.Prick with a fork.
  2. Mix all other ingradients in a flat dish. Add prawns coating them well.
  3. Cover and leave to mariate prawns in it for at least 1 hour in the fridge.
  4. Microwave covered for 3 minutes.
  5. Serve, garnished with lemon wedges and fresh coriander.
