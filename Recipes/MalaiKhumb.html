Malai Khumb

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 8 mins

Ingredients

  1. 200 gm mushrooms - big size
MARINADE
  1. 1/2 cup curd - hang in a muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  2. 1 tsp ginger paste
  3. 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  4. 2 tbsp thick cream
  5. 1 tsp oil
  6. 1/4 tsp garam masala
  7. 2 tbsp grated cheese
  8. 11/2 tsp corn flour
  9. 3/4 tsp salt
  10. 1 tbsp chat masala

Method

  1. Lightly squeeze hung curd and transfer to a bowl. Add all the other ingredieants of the marinade and mix well.
  2. Wash mushrooms and pat dry on a kitchen towel. Marinate mushrooms in this till serving time.
  3. At serving time, grease the non stick tawa. Place the mushrooms on it.
  4. Place the tawa on high rack and grill mushrooms for 8 mins.
  5. If the mushrooms are not golden brown, grill for 1-2 mins more.
  6. Serve sprinkled with chat masala.
