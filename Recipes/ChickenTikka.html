Chicken Tikka

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 16 mins

Ingredients

  1. 500 gm boneless chicken
1st MARINADE
  1. 2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  2. 1/4 tsp salt
  3. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  4. 2 tbsp oil
2nd MARINADE
  1. 3/4 cup curd - hang in muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  2. 2 tbsp thick malai or cream
  3. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1/4 tsp black salt
  5. 1 tbsp tandoori masala
  6. 2 tbsp corn flour
  7. 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 2-3 drops of red color
  10. 2 tbsp oil
  11. 1 tbsp chat masala

Method

  1. Cut chicken into 1-2" tikka pieces. Wash chicken pices and pat dry on a kitchen towel.
  2. Prick with a fork. Marinate in the 1st marinate for 1/2 hour in a flat dish.
  3. In a bowl mix all ingredients of 2nd marinade. Add only the chicken pieces disarding the liquid.
  4. Marinate for 2 hours or more. At serving time, grease the the non-stick tawa.
  5. Place the chicke pieces on it. Place the tawa on low rack and roast chicken for 15-20 mins at 180° till golden.
  6. Serve sprinikled with chaat masala.
