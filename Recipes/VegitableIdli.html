Vegitable Idli

Preparation: 1 hour
Cooking: 8 mins

Ingredients

  1. Idli batter
  2. 1/4 cup grated carrot
  3. 4 tbsp finely cut cabbage
  4. 4 tbsp finely cut beans
  5. 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  6. Finely cut coriander leaves
  7. 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  8. 1 tsp oil
  9. 1 tsp mustard seeds
  10. 1 tsp blackgram dal
  11. 1 tsp bengalgram dal
  12. Few curry leaves

Method

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, blackgram dal and bengalgram dal.
  2. When it splutters add curry leaves, carrot, beans, onions and cabbage. Roast for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add this and rest of the ingredients to the idli batter. Mix well and pour in idli plates.
  4. Steam for 7-8 minutes till done.
  5. Serve hot with chutney/sambar or nalla karam podi.
