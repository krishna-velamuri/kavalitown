Pav Bhaji

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 3 medium sized potatoes,1/2 cup peas
  2. 2 carrots-peeled and chopped
  3. 1/2 cups chopped cauliflower
  4. 1 cup chopped cabbage or 1 capsicum
  5. 3 tbsp oil
  6. 3 tbsp butter
  7. 2 onoins-chopped finely
  8. 1 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  9. 2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  10. 1/4 tsp turmeric powder(haldi)
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 1/2 cup tomoto puree
  13. 1 tbsp chopped coriander

Method

  1. Wet potatoes.Microwave patatoes covered or in a micro safe stemar for 5 minutes.
  2. Peel and mash coarsely.
  3. In a micro proof dish,put carrots,peas,cauliflower&cabbage or capsicum.Add 1/2 cup water.
  4. Cover and microwave for 5 minutes.Mash with a potato masher.
  5. Put oil,onions,ginger-garlic paste,2 tbsp pav bhaji masala and haldi.Mix well in a dish.
  6. Microwave for 5 minutes.
  7. Add tomato puree,roughly mashed vegetables,potatoes,butter and salt.Add 1 cup water.
  8. Cover and microwave for 5-6 minutes.Mash once in between.
