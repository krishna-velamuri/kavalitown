Eggless Chocolate Cake

Eggless Chocolate Cake

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 5 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 condensed milk(milkmaid)
  2. 1/2 cup milk
  3. 1 tsp vanilla essense
  4. 1/2 cup oil
  5. 4 tbsp powdered sugar
  6. 100 gm plain flour(maida)
  7. 1/4 cup cocoa
  8. 1/2 tsp soda
  9. 1 tsp baking powder
  10. chocolate sauce
  11. walnut halves

Method

  1. Sift maida, cocoa, soda and baking powder. Keep a side.
  2. Beat sugar and milkmaid till fluffy. Add oil. Beat well.
  3. Add milk and essense. Add maida.
  4. Beat well for 3-4 mins till the mixture is smooth and light.
  5. Take a round 6"-7" diameter microproof glass dish or plastic container.
  6. The container should have 4" height with straight sides.
  7. Line the container with brown paper. Grease paper with oil.
  8. Pour the cake batter in it. Microwave for 5 mins.
  9. Let it stand for 5 mins in the microwave. Let it cool.
  10. Transfer to a sarving platter.
  11. Pour chocolate sauce on in & decorate with walnut halves.
