Methi Chicken

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 15 1/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 400 gm chicken with bones
  2. 2 tbsp oil
  3. 350 gm fresh fenugreek leaves(methi leaves)(1 1/2 cups chopped)
  4. 1 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 1/2 cup milk/cream
  6. 1 cup curd-hang for 1 hour
ONION PASTE(Grind together)
  1. 1medium onion
  2. 2-3 flakes garlic
  3. 1/2" pice ginger
  4. 2-3 green chillies
  5. 2 tsp coriander powder
TADKA
  1. 1 tsp ghee
  2. 3/4 tsp red chilli powder
  3. 1/4 tsp pepper

Method

  1. In a dish,put 2 tbsp oil and onion paste.Mix.Microwave uncovered for 4 minutes.
  2. Add chicken,washed and squeezed methi leaves,curd and salt.Mix very well.
  3. Microwave uncovered for 8 minutes.stair and grill for 3 minutes.
  4. Let it stand for 2-3 minutes.Remove from oven and let it cool.
  5. Gradually add milk or cream,mixing continuously with the other hand.
  6. keep aside till serving time.
  7. Just before serving,in a small bowl microwave uncovered all ingredients of tadka for 15-20 seconds.
  8. Pour hot ghee over hot chicken.
