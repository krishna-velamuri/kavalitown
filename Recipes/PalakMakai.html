Palak Makai

Preparation: 20 mins
Cooking: 15 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 bundle (600 gm) spinach - chopped
  2. 1 cup corn (frozen or tin)
  3. 2 tbsp oil
  4. 1 tsp jeera
  5. 1 green elachi
  6. 1/2" piece ginger
  7. 4-5 flakes of garlic
  8. 2 onions - chopped
  9. 1 green chilli
  10. 1 tsp coriander powder
  11. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  12. 2 tomatoes - chopped
  13. 1 tbsp dry fenugreek leaves
  14. 1 tbsp butter
  15. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  16. 3/4 tsp salt
  17. 1/4 tsp sugar
  18. 1/4 cup milk

Method

  1. In a micro proof deep bowl put oil, jeera, elachi, ginger, garlic, onions, green chilli, dhania powder and garam masala.
  2. Mix well. Microwave for 5 mins. Add tomatoes and kasoori methi.
  3. Add washed spinach leaves. Microwave for 8 mins. Cool spinach. Blend with 1/2 cup water.
  4. Trasfer the spinach puree to the same micro proof dish.
  5. Add 1 tbsp butter, red chilli powder, corn, salt, sugar, 1/4 cup milk and 1/4 cup water.
  6. Mix well. Microwave covered for 2 mins. Serve hot.
