Tomato Rasam

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 11 mins

Ingredients

  1. 3 tomatoes
  2. A pinch of turmeric
  3. 1 tsp ready made rasam powder
  4. 1/2 tsp chopped garlic
  5. 1 tbsp jaggery(optional)
  6. 1 tbsp salt
  7. 1 tsp lemon juice or to taste
  8. ¼ tsp crushed black pepper
TEMPERING
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. A few curry leaves
  3. 1 tbsp mustard seeds (rai)
  4. ½ tbsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  5. A pinch of asafetida (hing)

Method

  1. Cover and microwave chopped tomatoes, 21/2 cups water, rasam powder, pasupu, garlic, salt and jaggery together for 7 minutes.
  2. Cool and puree. Strain puree.
  3. In a small bowl, put all the ingredients of tempering and microwave covered for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the tempering and also ¼ tbsp crushed pepper to the tomato puree and microwave for 2 minutes.
  5. Add lemon juice. Serve with papad.
