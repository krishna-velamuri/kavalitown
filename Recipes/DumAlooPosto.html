Sukhi Dhuli

Dum Aloo Posto

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 potatoes-peeled & scooped to from small balls or cut into 1" pieces
  2. 1 onion-chopped finely
  3. 3 tbsp mustarad oil or any other oil
PASTE
  1. 1 tbsp cashewnut(kaju)
  2. 2tbsp poppy seeds(gasalu)
  3. 1 green chilli-chopped
  4. 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  5. 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  6. 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  7. 1/4 tsp turmeric(pasupu)
  8. 1/4 cup water

Method

  1. Microwave all ingredients of the paste in a bowl for 1 minute.cool.
  2. Grind to a paste in a grinder till smooth.
  3. Microwave oil and onion in the dish uncovered for 5 minutes.
  4. Add paste and potatoes.Mix.Microwave for 2 minutes.
  5. Add 1/2 cup water and microwave covered for 3 minutes.Give standing time of 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven.Serve.
