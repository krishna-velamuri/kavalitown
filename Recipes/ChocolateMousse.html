Chocolate Mousse

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 31/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup water
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. 21/2 tbsp custard powder
  4. 2 tsp gelatine sprinkled over 1/4 cup water
  5. 125 gm cooking chocolate - cut into small pieces(1 cup)
  6. 200 gm cream
DECORATION
  1. 1 tbsp chocolate pieces
  2. 2 tbsp whipped cream (optional)
  3. Few glace cherries

Method

  1. Mix custard powder, sugar and water in a deep bowl till smooth.
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir once and microwave again for 1 min to get a thick sauce like consistency.
  4. Add chocolate to the hot sauce and stir well smooth.
  5. Microwave soaked gelatine for 30 sec. Pour hot genatine mixture in the hot chocolate mix.
  6. Stir well to mix. Keep a side to cool.
  7. Beat cream till fluffy. Add cream to the cooled chocolate mixture.
  8. Put in glasses or a serving bowl. Keep in the refregerator for 3-4 hours to set.
  9. Decorate with chocolate, cream and glace cherries.
