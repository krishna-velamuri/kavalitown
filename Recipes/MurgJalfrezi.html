Murg Jalfrezi

Preparation: 20 mins
Cooking: 12 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg boneless chicken breast-cut into thin long strips
  2. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  3. 1 tbsp oil
  4. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1 capsicum-cut into strips
  7. 1/4 cup cream or malai(optional)
PASTE(GRIND TOGETHER)
  1. 3 tomotoes
  2. 1tbsp tomoto ketchup
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  5. 1/2 tsp cumin(jeera)
  6. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  7. 1/4 tsp haldi(pasupu)
  8. 1 tsp coriander(dhania) powder
  9. 1/4 tsp pepper(optional)
  10. 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. .Marinate chicken in lemon juice,ginger-garlic paste,1 tbsp oil and 1/2 tsp salt.Keep aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Grind together all the ingredients given under paste in a mixer till smooth.Microwave raste for 6 minutes in a dish.
  3. dd the chicken with the marinade and capsicum.Microwave covered for 6 minutes.
  4. Give standing time of 3 minutes.
  5. Add cream. Mix. Ganesh with chopped coriander leaves and garam masala.Serve hot.
