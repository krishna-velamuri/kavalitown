Baingan Bharta

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 19 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1medium(350 gm) brinjal of round variety
  2. 2tbsp oil
  3. 2 onions -chopped finelly
  4. 1" piece ginger-chopped finely(1 tbsp)
  5. 1 green chilli-chopped
  6. 2 tsp coriander(dhania)powder
  7. 1/4 tsp garam masala
  8. 1/2 tsp degi mirch or red chilli powder
  9. 3/4 tsp salt
  10. a pinch of haldi(pasupu)
  11. 2 tbsp ready-made tomoto puree
  12. 1 tomoto-chopped finely

Method

  1. Wash brinjal.prick with a fork.place wet brinjal in a micro proof flat dish.Microwave for 5 minutes.
  2. Let it cool down. Cut into half and scoop out the pulp with a spoon.
  3. cut the pulp with a knife.Mash pulp.Keep pulp aside.
  4. In the same dish,put oil,onions,ginger,green chilli,dhania powder,garam masala,degi mirch.
  5. Add brinjal pulp.Mix well and microwave uncovered for 8 minutes.
  6. Add chopped tomoto,tomoto puree and 3/4 tsp salt.Mix well.Microwave covered for 6 minutes.Serve hot.
