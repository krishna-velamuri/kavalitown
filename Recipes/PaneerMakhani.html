Paneer Makhani

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 200 gm paneer - cut into pieces
  2. 5 large (500 gm) tomatoes - chopped
  3. 1 tsp chopped ginger
  4. 2 tbsp butter/ghee
  5. 2 tbsp oil
  6. 2 elachi - crushed
  7. 1/2 tsp sugar
  8. 1 tsp salt or to taste
  9. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  10. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  11. 1 tsp tomato kethup
  12. 4 tbsp cashew nuts or magaz - soak in 1/4 cup wate and ground to paste
  13. 2 tsp dry fenugreek leaves
  14. 1/2 cup milk or cream

Method

  1. In a deep dish cover and microwave tomatoes, ginger and 1/2 cup water for 5 mins.
  2. Cool. Puree in a mixer. Microwave butter/ghee, oil and elachi for 2 mins.
  3. Add salt, sugar, red chilli powder, and garam masala. mix.
  4. Add fresh tomato puree and tomato ketchup. Mix very well.
  5. Microwave covered for 4 mins. Stir once. Microwave uncovered for 4 mins again.
  6. Add cashew nut paste and kasoori methi. Mix well. Add 1/2 cup water.
  7. Microwave for 3 mins. Add paneer. Add enough milk/cream to get a thick red gravy.
  8. Microwave for 2 mins. Serve.
