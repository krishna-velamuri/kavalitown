Aam Panna

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 250 gm green mangoes
  2. ½ cup powdered sugar
  3. ¼ cup mint leaves (poodina)
  4. 1 tsp roasted cumin(sompu) powder
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. A pinch of black salt

Method

  1. Peel raw mangoes. Chop roughly.
  2. Place mangoes in a deep micro proof bowl with 6 cups of water and microwave covered for 10 min.
  3. When cool, blend in mixer.
  4. Blend the mint leaves separately to a smooth paste in a small spice grinder with a little water.
  5. Add mint, cumin powder, powdered sugar and salt to mango puree. Mix well.
  6. Take a muslin cloth or a very fine metal strainer and strain the mixture.
  7. Check and adjust the seasoning with chilli
  8. Pour the panna over ice and garnish with a mint spring.
