Chocolate Cake

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 25 mins

Ingredients

  1. 4 large eggs
  2. 1 cup powdered sugar
  3. 1 cup maida
  4. 1/2 cup cocoa
  5. 3/4 level tsp baking powder
  6. a pinch of mitha soda
  7. 3/4 cup oil
  8. 1 tsp vanilla essence

Method

  1. Sieve flour, cocoa, baking powder and mitha soda together.
  2. Beat sugar and eggs very well together and add essence.
  3. Add oil gradually beating mixure lightly.
  4. Fold in flour a little at a time, with a wooden spoon, till all the flour is used up.
  5. Pour into a greased baking tin and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 25 mins.
  6. Let it cool for 5-10 mins before removing from tin.
  7. Remove from tin and keep on wire rack for a few minutes.
Note: For plain cake, remove cocoa and add 1/2 cup corn flour instead.
