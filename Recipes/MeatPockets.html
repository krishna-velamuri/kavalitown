Meat Pockets

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 thick pizza bases or pita bread
  2. 4 sausages - thinly sliced diagonally
  3. 1 cup corn kernels (forzen or tinned)
  4. 2 capsicums
  5. 1 onion - sliced thinly
  6. 2-3 tbsp tomato puree
  7. 2 tbsp oil (olive)
  8. a few lettuce or cabbage leaves - shredded
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
TOPPING
  1. 1 tbsp butter
  2. 100 grm pizza cheese - grated (1 cup)
  3. 1 tbsp red chilli flakes
  4. 1 tsp oregano

Method

  1. Put oil and sausages in dish. Mix well.
  2. Add corn, onion, capsicum, tomato puree, pepper and a pinch of salt. Microwave for 3 minutes.
  3. Cut pizza base into half. Split open the cut side with the tip of a knife to make a deep pocket.
  4. Butter top of the pizza halves. Push lettuce or cabbage into the pocket.
  5. Fill the pizza pockets with sausage mixture.
  6. Place pizza pieces on the low rack. Sprinkle cheese, chilli flakes and oregano.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 8 minutes or till base gets crisp and cheese melts.
  8. Cut each half into 2 pieces. Serve.
