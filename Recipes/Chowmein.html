Chowmein

Preparation: 20 min
Cooking: 15 mins

Ingredients

  1. 100 gm noodles
  2. 1 onion - cut into thin slices
  3. 1 capsicum - shredded into thin strips
  4. 1 carrot - matchsticks or juliennes
  5. 1 cup shredded cabbage
  6. 3/4 tsp salt
  7. 1 tsp white pepper
  8. a pinch of sugar
  9. 1/4 tsp ajinomoto - optional
  10. 1-2 tsp soya sauce
  11. 1/2 tsp vinegar
  12. 11/2 tsp chilli sauce
  13. 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Microwave covered 3 cups of water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tbsp oil in a deep bowl for 6 mins or till boil.
  2. Add noodles. Mix well. Microwave uncovered for 2 mins. Mix again. Let it be in hot water for 4-5 mins.
  3. Strain. Sprinkle 1 tbsp oil on it. Spread on a greased tray under the fan.
  4. In a flat dish microwave 1 tbsp oil, onions, carrot and capsicum for 5 mins.
  5. Add cabbage, salt, pepper, sugar and ajinomoto. Mix well.
  6. Add noodles, vinegar, chilli sauce and soya sauce. Mix well and microwave for 2 mins.
  7. Stirring once in between. Serve.
