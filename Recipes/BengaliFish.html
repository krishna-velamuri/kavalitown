Bengali Fish Curry

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 81/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 300 gm fish - cut into 11/2" flat pieces
  2. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  5. 4 tbsp oil
  6. 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  7. 1 tbsp chopped coriander
MUSTARD PASTE
  1. 4 tsp yellow mustard
  2. 2 dry chillies - deseed
  3. 1 onion - chopped finely
  4. 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 2-3 tbsp water to grind

Method

  1. Rub lemon juice, salt and haldi on fish. Keep aside for 10 mins.
  2. Wash fish, pat dry. Spread 4 tbsp oil in a dish. Place fish in the dish in a single layer.
  3. Turn side, so that both sides of the fish are coated with oil.
  4. Microwave for 3 mins. Add 1/2 cup water. Microwave for 3 mins.
  5. Add fish. Mix well. Add 1/4 tsp chilli powder. Sprinkle chopped coriander.
  6. keep aside till serving time. To serve microwave for 1 min.
