Gajarka Halwa

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 31/2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg carrots - peeled and grated
  2. 1/2 cup milk
  3. 1/2 cup sugar
  4. 1/2 cup khova - roughly mashed
  5. 2 tbsp ghee
  6. 2 green cardmom
  7. 1 tbsp chopped nuts like almonds or cashews
  8. 2 tbsp whipped cream (optional)
  9. 1 tbsp kishmish

Method

  1. Mix grated carrots, sugar and milk in a big deep bowl.
  2. Microwave covered for 10 mins.
  3. Add ghee and khova. Mix well.
  4. Microwave uncovered for 10 mins.
  5. Add cardmom powder and chopped nuts.
  6. Serve hot decorate with nuts.
