Tamota Chutney

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 kg tomatoes - chopped finely
  2. 1/2 onion - chopped
  3. 2 thsp chopped garlic
  4. 1" piece ginger - grated
  5. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. 1 tsp salt
  7. 1/4 tsp pepper
  8. 1/2 cup sugar
  9. 1/4 cup vinegar
  10. 1 tbsp kishmish - cleaned
  11. 2 almonds (badam) - cut finely
  12. 5 green elachi
BAGHAR
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. 1/2 tsp urad dhal(black gram)
  3. 1/2 tsp chenna dhal
  4. 1/2 tsp sesam seeds(Nuvvulu)

Method

  1. Mix onion, garlic and ginger together in a dish. Microwave for 3 mins.
  2. Add tomatoes, red chilli powder, salt, pepper, sugar and vinegar. Mix well.
  3. Microwave for 10 mins. Mash well.
  4. Add cleaned raisins, almonds and elachi powder. Microwave for 5 mins.
  5. Microwave oil, dals, sesame seeds and curry leaves in a small bowl uncovered for 2 mins.
  6. Pour the hot oil on the chutney. Cool slightly and fill while warm in sterilized bottles.
  7. Cover with a lid after it cools.
