Butter Chicken

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 19 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 medium sized chicken (800 grm) - cut into 12 pieces (cut into 11/2-2" tikka pieces)
  1. 1" piece ginger and 10 flakes garlic - chopped
  2. 1/2 cup cashew nuts
  3. 2 cups ready-made tomato puree
  4. 1 tsp garam masala
  5. 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  6. 1/4 tsp sugar or to taste
  7. 3/4 tsp salt
  8. 2 tbsp butter
  9. 2-3 tbsp oil
  10. 1/2 cup milk
  11. 1/4 cup cream

Method

  1. Grind ginger, garlic and cashews to a paste in a mixer with a little water.
  2. Put paste in a deep dish. Add tomato puree, all masalas, oil and butter.
  3. Microwave covered for 8 mins. Add about 1 cup of water, mix and microwave covered for 6 mins.
  4. Stir once in between. Add cooked chicken to the gravy. Cover and microwave for 3 mins.
  5. Remove from oven and let it cool down till servin time.
  6. Add milk and microwave uncovered for 2 mins. Add cream. Serve hot.
