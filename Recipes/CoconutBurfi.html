Coconut Burfi

Preparation: 5 mins
Cooking: 2 mins

Ingredients

  1. 11/2 cup coconut powder
  2. 1/2 tin condenced milk (milkmaid)
  3. a few drops pink color
  4. a silver sheet (optional)

Method

  1. In a flat dish spread coconut powder and microwave for 2 mins to dry roast the coconut.
  2. Add milkmaid. Mix well. Devide in to two parts.
  3. To one part add pink color.
  4. Spread white part in he tin. Top with the pink part.
  5. Decorate with silver sheet.
  6. Refrigerate for 1 hour. Cut into pieces for serving.
