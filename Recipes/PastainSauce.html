Pasta in Tomato Sauce

Preparation: 10 min
Cooking: 23 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups penne or macaroni or other shape pasta
  2. 1 medium green or yellow capsicum - cut into thin strips
  3. 1 medium potato - cut into 4 pieces and remove pulp, cut into strips
  4. 3 tbsp olive oil
  5. 2 tsp crushed garlic
  6. 1 cup ready-made tomato puree
  7. a few back olives
  8. 1/2 tsp pepper
  9. 11/4 tsp salt
  10. 3/4 tsp oregano or mixed herbs
  11. 1/2 cup water
  12. 1/2 cup cream or milk

Method

  1. Microwave covered 6 cups of water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tbsp oil in a deep bowl for 10 mins.
  2. Add pasta. Microwave for 4 mins. Stri in between. Let it stand in hot water for 2-3 mins.
  3. Strain. Sprinkle 1 tbsp olive oil on it.
  4. Microwave 2 tbsp oil, crushed garlic, capsicum, ready-made tomato puree, pepper, oregano and salt for 6 mins.
  5. Add boiled pasta, Mix well. Keep aside till serving time. To serve, add tomato, cream or milk and water to pasta.
  6. Mix well. Microwave for 3 mins. Add olives. Check salt and pepper. Serve hot with garlic.
  7. Stirring once in between. Serve.
