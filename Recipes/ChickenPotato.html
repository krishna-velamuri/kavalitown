Chicken Potato pie

Preparation: 12 min
Cooking: 34 mins

Ingredients

  1. 250 gm boneless chicken - cut into 1/2" pieces
  2. 4 potatoes
  3. Salt, pepper to taste
  4. 2 tbsp chopped parsley or coriander
  5. 3 tbsp butter
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. 1/2 packet redy-made vegitable soup
  8. 1/2 cup grated cheese 50 gm)
  9. 1/4 tsp pepper corns - crushed
  10. a few slices of black olives

Method

  1. Microwave wet potatoes on a micro proof plate in a ring for 6 mins.
  2. Cool, peel and grate potatoes. Add 3 tbsp butter, parsley, 1/4 tsp salt and 1/4 pepper.
  3. In a flat dish cover and microwave 1 tbsp olive oil, chicken, 1/4 tsp and and pepper for 3 mins.
  4. In a bowl, dissolve 1/2 cup packet soup in 11/4 cups of water. Microwave for 6 mins.
  5. Spread out chicken in the baking dish. pour the thick soup over the chicken.
  6. Sprinkle cheese, pepper and olives. Bake at 200°C for 20 mins.
  7. Grill for 2 mins for potatoes to turn golden from the top. Serve.
