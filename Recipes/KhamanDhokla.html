Khaman Dhokla

Preparation: 10 mins
Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups garam flour(besan)
  2. 2 tbsp semolina(suji)
  3. 1 1/4 cups water
  4. 1 tbsp oil
  5. 1/4 tsp baking soda(mitha soda)
  6. 1 1/4 tsp eno fruit salt
  7. 1 tbsp salt
  8. 2 tsp sugar
  9. 1/4 tsp turmeric(haldi)
  10. 1 tsp green chilli paste
  11. 1 tsp ginger paste
  12. 2 tsp lemon juice
TEMPERING
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. 1 tsp mustard seeds(rai)
  3. 2-3 green chillies-slit into long pieces
  4. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  5. 3/4 cup water
  6. 3 tsp sugar
  7. 2 tbsp finely chopped coriendder

Method

  1. Wash and put wet potatoes in a micro safe steamer .Microwave covered for 5 minutes.Cool.
  2. Peel and mash potatoes.Add oil,peas,salt,jeere,chopped green chillies,ginger,coriander, red chilli powder,chaat masala and garam masala.
  3. Make tikkis of 3" diameter.
  4. Brush the non stick tawa of the microwave with some oil.Arrange tikkis on it.
  5. Brush the tikkis with oil.Place the tawa on a high rack.
  6. Grill for 10 minutes.Turn side and grill for 4 minutes.
