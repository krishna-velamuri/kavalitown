Banana Muffins

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 22 mins

Ingredients

  1. 60 gm (1/3 cup) softned salter butter
  2. 1/3 cup milk
  3. 1 tsp vanilla essence
  4. 1 small riped banana - pureed in a small grinder
  5. 55 gm (1/2 cup) whole wheat flour
  6. 55 gm (1/2 cup) plain flour (maida)
  7. 3/4 tsp baking powder
  8. 1 egg
  9. 100 gm (3/4 cup) powdered sugar
  10. 1/4 cup blue berry jam
  11. paper cups - optional

Method

  1. Line muffin tin with greased cups.
  2. Beat egg till fluffy. Sift both flours with baking powder and keep aside.
  3. Microwave butter and milk for 2 mins in a big bowl. Add assense, sugar and banana puree beat till creamy.
  4. Add flour and beaten egg. Fold gently with a spoon to get a lumpy batter of a soft dropping consistency. Do not over mix.
  5. Spoon batter into paper cups, filling half of the cups. Spoon 1 tbsp jam. Cover with some more batter.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 25 mins in a pre-heated oven.
