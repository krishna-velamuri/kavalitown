Chicken Lasagne

Preparation: 10 min
Cooking: 1 hour

Ingredients

  1. 5 broad sheets of lasagne
  2. 3 chicken breast bone less
  3. 1 cup water
  4. 1/2 cup corn
  5. 100 gm mozzarella cheese - gratted(1 cup)
  6. 3 tbso tomato ketchup
  7. 1 tomato - finely chopped
  8. 1/2 tsp salt
SAUCE
  1. 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  2. 1/2 tsp salt
  3. 3/4 tsp oregano or mixed herbs
  4. 3 tbsp olive oil
  5. 4 tbsp flour
  6. 11/2 cup milk
  7. 1 chicken soup cube - crushed

Method

  1. Cover and microwave chicken, salt and water for 6 mins Reserve stok.
  2. Cut chicken into pieces. Mix tomato and ketchup with chicken.
  3. Mix 3 tbsp olive oil with flour. Microwave for 2 mins. Stir immediately.
  4. add stock from step 1. Add remaining ingredients of sauce. Microwave for 6 mins, till thick. Stir in between.
  5. Spread 3 tbsp sauce in baking dish. Dip a lasagne sheet in hot water and lay in the dish.
  6. Spoon sauce to cover. Sprinkle chicken corn and mozzarella cheese. Repeat till all sheets are used up.
  7. Top with remaining sauce, covering all sides. Sprinkle cheese and corn.
  8. Cover dish with oil and bake for 45 mins in preheated oven at 180°C. Remove foil. Grill for 2 mins.
