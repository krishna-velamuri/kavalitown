Coconut Chutney

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 3 mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup freshly grated coconut - remove brown skin before gratting
  2. 2-3 green chillies
  3. 1/2" piece of ginger
  4. salt to taste
TEMPERING
  1. 1 tbsp oil
  2. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  3. 1 dry chilli - broken small pices
  4. few curry leaves

Method

  1. Grind coconut, green chillies and ginger together, by adding enough water to make thick paste.
  2. Add a little salt to taste. Remove chutney into a serving bowl.
  3. Microwave oil, saron, red chilli and curry leaves in a small bowl uncovered for 3 mins.
  4. Pour the hot oil on the chutney. Serve chutney with dosas, idlis and vadas.
