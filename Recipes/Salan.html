Hyderabadi Salan

Preparation: 15 mins
Cooking: 21 mins

Ingredients

  1. 5-6 thick green chillies
  2. 1 thin and long brinjal
  3. 3 tbsp roasted peanuts
  4. 1 tbsp vinegar
  5. some curry leaves
  6. 3 tbsp oil
  7. 1 tsp jeera
  8. 1 tsp mustard seeds
  9. 2 onions - grounded to paste
  10. 2 tsp coriander powder
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  13. 1/2 tsp dry mango powder
  14. 1/4 tsp garam masala
  15. 1 cup ready-made tomato puree
  16. 1/2 tsp ginger paste

Method

  1. Grind peanuts with 1/2 cup water to a paste.
  2. Silt chillies, remove seeds. Rub 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tbsp vinegar.
  3. Cut brinjal into round slices, sprinkle salt. After 15 mins wash and pat dry vegetables.
  4. Mix 1 tbsp oil, brinjal and chillies. Spread on a greased tawa. Put tawa on high rack. Grill for 5 mins.
  5. Microwave 2 tbsp oil, jeera, curry leaves, ginger paste and onions with all spices together for 7 mins.
  6. Add tomato puree. Microwave for 4 mins.
  7. Add peanut paste, 1 cup water, brinjal and mirchi. Microwave for 5 mins. Serve.
