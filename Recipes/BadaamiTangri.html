Badaami Tangri

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 12 mins

Ingredients

  1. 5 drumsticks (500 gm chicken legs)
1st MARINADE
  1. 2 tbsp lemon juice
  2. 1/2 tsp salt
  3. 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  4. 1 tbsp oil
2nd MARINADE
  1. 1/2 cup curd - hang in muslin cloth for 1/2 hour
  2. 8-10 almonds - ground to a powder
  3. 2 tbsp oil
  4. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. 1/2 garam masala powder
  6. 1 tsp salt
  7. 1/2 tsp pepper
  8. 1 tbsp corn flour

Method

  1. Rub the fish well with gramflour and lemon juice to remove fishy odour.
  2. Keep aside for 15 mins. Wash well and pat dry on kitchen towel.
  3. Prick fish all over with a fork. Marinate in 1st marinade for 1/2 hour.
  4. In a bowl mix all the ingredients of the 2nd marinade. Add fish. Refregirate for 1-2 hours.
  5. At serving time, cover rack with foil. Grease it. Place tikkas on it.
  6. Grill for 8 mins, turning once in between.
