About Kavali

Kavali is a big town in Nellore District on East coast. It is positioned on NH5 and South Central Railway[Howra-chennai] line. Kavali means patrol. There are many storis behind this name. Following are the two main stories...

Emperor Sri Krishna Devaraya had stationed his army at this place to protect the udayagiri fort, because of this, named Kavali.

Because of costal area and well connected, people use to go through this area. The merchents stationed some security (patrol) to protect their imports. Because of this, named Kavali.

The municipality formed in 1967 April 21 as a grade III, now it is a Grade I (currently Grade II, very soon It will be Grade I) Municipality with 110000 population. It became a educational hub, town has 5 Engineering, 5 P.G, lot of Degree, Intermediate, Nursing,1 polytechnic, 1 B.Ed colleges. In soon Medical college also going to establish.

The total area of the town is 67sqkm. Kavali has another name, mini bombay becuse of whole sale cloth business. Kavali is a mandal, Taluk and big revenue division in nellore district til atmakur revenue formed(recenty). It is located border of nellore and prakasam districts. Very peaceful town. Most of the people are in agriculture and dairy profession. Somany great persons came from this town. [1]

Geography

Kavali is located at 14.92°N and 79.98°E. It has an average elevation of 17 metres (56 ft). The elevation from east to west. Due to this elevation water problem occured in II town. In I town we will get ground water within 3 meters of depth, 20-40 meters in case of II town. The geographical area has several kinds of soils, like gravel, rocky, sand, klay, red soil etc. The rainfall is heigh in Oct - Nov months due to cyclones. Most of the cyclones are reached kavali. Kavali revenue has huge govt lands in andhra pradesh.

History

The role of kavali is very less in hisory. Udayagiri is the capital of this place most of the times.In 1904 for swadhesi concept Shri. Oruganti Varadhaih started swadeshi article shop in kavali. In 1916 for Shri. Tilak home rule concept a branch has started in kavali along with nellore. In 1930 as part of salt march, leaders did salt march at Thummalapenta.

Political

Kavali assembly constitution has formed in the year 1951. Intially kavali is assembly and parliment constitution in nellore district. Kavali parliment has rolled out in the year 1977 due to the delimination based on the population. At this time Ongole is in nellore district, when kavali parliment rolled out Ongole became as parliment constitution for kavali. Till 2009 kavali is under Ongole parliment, now it is part of Nellore parliment.

Economy

Agricutlure and dairy products are the main source of income. Textile business is the other main source of income. Paddy production and exports are in the third place. Nearly there are 40 + rice mills in town. They will produce boiled rice, raw rice and other paddy related products.

Textil is main business in the town, nearly 10000 people are depends on it. The anual trunover is 400 crores. Here many readymade garments are there like Kandukuri, Independen, Arc, Spanish, Marvel..etc

Education

The town has been playing a key role on education stream in Andhra pradesh. Many VIP's had studied at kavali, like Varavara Rao, Dodla Ramachandra Reddy, etc... There are 5 Engineering, 5 P.G, 5 Degree, 10 Intermediate, Nursing, polytechnic, B.Ed and lot of corporate schools/colleges are there. I think very soon Medical college also going to establish.

Transport

Town has very good transport fecilities like road, rail, air and water ways. The Grand Trunk Road (NH5) goes through heart of the city. South Central Railway main line (Chennai - Howrah) divides town in to two parts. Proposed nellore airport 30km from kavali. Chennai aiport and fort are 220km away. Vijayawada airport is 230km. Krishnapatnam port 60km from town. Huge Ramayapatnam fort is jut 12 km from kavali. The proposed NW4 (National Water Way - Bakinghom Canal is 6km away.

Many Express, Superfast trains has stop at kavali. There are lot of buses, which are connecting all major cities of south india like Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Vijayawada..etc
