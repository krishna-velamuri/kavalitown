Kavali Revenue

Revenue Division

Kavali is a largest revenue divison (with 19 mandal's) in Nellore district till 2013. There were 3 revenue divisions in Nellore district before 2013. In 2013 two more revenue divisions are added (Atmakuru, Naidupet). Some of the Kavali revenue mandals are separated for Atmakuru revenue division like Udayagiri, Vinjamur, Seetharamapuram.etc.
