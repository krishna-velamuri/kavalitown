Indore Stadium In 2001 CM Chandrababu Naidu announced mini stadium with swimming pool. In 2014 tenders called for stadium construction with 3.9 crores value on 15-2-2014. (No.23/APMSIDC/2013-14 ). People are looking forward for Stadium.
Ramayapatnam Port Ramayapatnam Port will be a new major port to be built at Ramayapatnam. It will be the second major port in Andhra Pradesh after the Visakhapatnam Port. This is just 12km from kavali. It will help kavali to grow like a city.
Aqua Research Centre The Central Government has agreed in principle to establish Aqua research centre at Kavali in Nellore district. The Centre has asked the State to acquire necessary land for establishing the centres.
Nellore Airport Nellore Airport is being planned to be built near Dagadarthi, 32 km from Kavali. It is one of the 50 locations identified by the Central Government of India for the development of low-cost airports by the AAI in order to improve connectivity to remote areas.
Nuclear Reactor This is not at all required by Kavali People. The government announced that Russian owned Rosatom will build its next phase of six reactors in Andhra Pradesh. Sources told The Hindu the project site identified, believed to be Kavali in Nellore district.
