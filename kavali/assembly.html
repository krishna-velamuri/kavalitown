Assembly Constitution

Kavali assembly constitution has formed in the year 1951. Intially kavali is a assembly and parliment constitution in nellore district. Kavali parliment has rolled out in the year 1977 due to the delimination based on the population. At this time Ongole is in nellore district. When kavali parliment rolled out, Ongole became as parliment constitution. Till 2009 kavali is under Ongole parliment, now it is part of Nellore parliment.

Kavali assembly is unique.

Mandals in Kavali assembly: Kavali, Bogole, Allur and Dagadarthi Mandals.

In town the political dominent casts are Reddy, Naidu and Yadava.
Famous politician: Kaliki Yanadhi Reddy
Famous Person: Potti Sriramulu
